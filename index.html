Instructions

Here are the instructions for the memory game:

  1. The game board will display a grid of facedown cards. Click on any card to reveal its number.
  2. Try to remember the numbers and their positions.
  3. Click on another card to reveal its number. If the numbers match, the cards will stay face up. Otherwise, they will be flipped face down again.
  4. Continue selecting cards and matching pairs until all cards are revealed.
  5. Once you have matched all the pairs, a congratulatory message will appear with your completion time.
  6. If you want to play again, click the "Play Again" button.

Difficulty Levels:

  • Easy: The cards' positions will change every 2 minutes.
  • Medium: The cards' positions will change every 10 seconds.
  • Hard: The cards' positions will change every 5 seconds.

Choose the difficulty level that suits your preference and challenge yourself!

Have fun and enjoy the game!
